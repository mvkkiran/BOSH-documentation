Abstract

This document explains the ontology for the smart home and smart building domain. This ontology covers wide variety of the concepts that are popularly used in the aforementioned domain. The ontology contains various concepts related to sensors, actuators, measurements, electrical and electronic appliances, building environment such as building, floor, flat, room, wall and, ceiling. This ontology is an outcome of the paper titled "Knowledge extraction using semantic similarity of concepts from Web of Things knowledge bases" and is currently under review. Smart Home and Building Ontology (BOSH) is developed as part of the PhD research work.

Smart Home and Building Ontology (BOSH): Description back to ToC

BOSH ontology incorporates most commonly used concepts across the smart home and smart building domain. The popular concepts are extracted from the mentioned domains using the framework proposed in the paper titled "Knowledge extraction using semantic similarity of concepts from Web of Things knowledge bases". BOSH is still evolving and will undergo multiple revisions. Please reach out to the authors incase if you find some discrepencies or to add any concepts that are missing.
